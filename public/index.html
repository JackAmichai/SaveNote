How SaveNote Works

Three simple steps to never forget anything again.

1

Send a Message

Send yourself a WhatsApp message with anything you want to remember — a parking spot, book title, or a random idea.

2

AI Categorizes

Your local AI model instantly analyzes the message, extracts key details, and files it under the right category.

3

Recall Anytime

Ask "Where did I park?" via WhatsApp or search the dashboard. Your memories are always just a message away.

Get started with SaveNote

Clone the repository, run locally with Ollama, and start saving memories through WhatsApp in minutes.
